# Example Discovery Notes

Client: Acme Dental Group

Business goal: automate patient intake and reduce manual front-desk data entry.

Requested work:

- Online intake forms.
- CRM sync.
- Status dashboard.
- Staff training.

Constraints:

- Launch requested in 6 weeks.
- Buyer can approve under USD 25,000 without board review.
- HIPAA concerns must be addressed.
- Current CRM API capabilities are unclear.

Prompt:

Turn these discovery notes into a priced proposal with three tiers, assumptions, exclusions, timeline, acceptance criteria, and an internal risk review.
